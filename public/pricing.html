Frequently asked

How does the 1.5× cost markup work?

We pass through the underlying LLM inference cost (OpenAI, Ollama, local) and add 50% margin. If OpenAI charges $1 for your usage, you pay $1.50. This covers infrastructure, support, and the marketplace you can sell your own agents in.

What happens if I exceed my quota?

On Pro and Enterprise, overage is billed at the per-1k rate in your plan. On Free, you get a 402 with an upgrade link. We never silently drop requests — every call gets an explicit response with quota headers X-Quota-Used and X-Quota-Limit.

Can I bring my own LLM provider?

Yes. POST /v1/aaas/providers registers your OpenAI / Ollama / Azure OpenAI keys. The router auto-selects based on cost, latency, and capability. For Enterprise, we support on-prem and custom routing policies.

How do I get an API key?

Sign up at azurdesk.ai/signup, then POST /v1/api-keys with a JWT session. The key is shown once. For automated provisioning of multiple keys, see the API docs.

What's MCP and why does it matter?

MCP (Model Context Protocol) is the open standard for connecting AI agents to tools and data sources. Our MCP 1.0 server lets Claude Desktop, Cursor, Cline, and any MCP-compatible client consume your AzurDesk agents natively. No glue code.

Is there a free trial of Pro?

Yes — every new account gets 30 days of Pro free. We don't ask for a card upfront. At the end of the trial, you fall back to Free automatically unless you add a payment method.
